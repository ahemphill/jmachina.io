THE PROBLEM

WARNING: FIRM_POWER_IN_LOW_LIGHT

Stop choosing the wrong tech for the wrong lux. The industry optimizes for bright windows and unrealistic use cases.

The 155 Lux Reality: Datasheets claim performance at 200 lux. Real devices live in 155 lux (avg) and 1000 lux use cases. That's a -85% power delta before you even start.

The "Lab-to-Fab" Gap: A prototype on a bench is not a product. Without an ecosystem strategy that accounts for lens loss, PMIC efficiency, and storage leakage, your battery-free ML model is just a paperweight.

The "Always-On" Myth: Intelligence at the edge requires continuous power. Most harvesters brown out during the ~15 hours of daily darkness while powering local inference. We provide the data and the power architecture to bridge the gap.
